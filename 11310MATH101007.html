教學進度 (Schedule)

原則上2~3週教1個章節。下表隨時間更新。

  • Week1(9/3,9/5): introduction, def of lim, lim laws, lim of quotient
  • Week2(遠距教學): lim of quotient, pinching thm, trigonometric lim, continuity
  • Week3(遠距教學): intermediate value thm, extreme value thm, 9/17 is a holiday
  • Week4(9/24, 9/26): derivative, differentiation rules
  • Week5(10/1, 10/3): chain rule, derivatives of trigonometric functions, implicit differentiation
  • Week6(10/8): Exam1(10/8), 10/10 is a holiday
  • Week7(10/15,10/17): mean value thm, monotone function, max/min, curve sketching
  • Week8(10/22,10/24): L’Hôpital’s rule, integration, fundamental thm of calculus
  • Week9(10/29,10/31): integration by substitution, integration by parts
  • Week10(11/5,11/7): applications of integration, inverse functions
  • Week11(11/12,11/14): Exam2(11/12), log, exp
  • Week12(11/19,11/21): inverse trigonometric functions, hyperbolic functions
  • Week13(11/26,11/28): techniques of integration, integration of rational functions
  • Week14(12/3,12/5): integration of rational functions, differential equations
  • Week15(12/10,12/12): differential equations
  • Week16(12/17,12/19): Final exam(12/17), no class on 12/19
